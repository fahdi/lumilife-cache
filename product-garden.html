Garden Light

LumiLife outdoor LED garden light LM-G001005 is very popular now. The beam angle of this led landscape lights is 60 degree, so it can use as LED garden spot light, too. LM-G001005 has a spike to insert in the ground to project colorful lights to trees, flowers, lawns,etc. And input voltage of the this led exterior light is 100-240Vac. But 12V garden light is also available in Lumilife Lighting. Besides, blue led lights are provided for LM-G001005.
  • Salient Features
  • 3 in 1: Landscape, in-ground and underwater light in one..
  • Stainless steel brackets and spikes
  • Aluminum Alloy , Tempered Glass
  • Water Proof, ip68
  • 5 Year Warranty
  • Best Cost Performance ratio
